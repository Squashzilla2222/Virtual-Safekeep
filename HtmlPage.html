Password strength tester


On this page, you can check the strength of your passwords based on how long it is and what characters it includes - any passwords under 8 characters are automatically defined as weak. Strong passwords should include capital letters, numbers, special characters, and be over 8 characters long. This feature is to help you use unique and secure passwords for your accounts, as well as checking if existing passwords hold a high standard.
